Qorona Team

The Qorona Team is proud to present to you what we believe is a strong tool to overcome the virus that has taken the world by surprise. We have worked on this project for months now, and we are ready to fight COVID-19 using a combination of creativity, artificial intelligence, and perseverance.

We are a group of 3 high-schoolers with ambitious dreams to use artificial intelligence to try and reduce the ever-lasting strain on our national hospitals. Made up of 2 programmers and one designer and researcher, we have combined our skills to make Qorona


Why use Qorona?

Qorona uses brand-new, state-of-the-art technology in order to help reduce the spread of COVID-19. Here are some of the features you get the luxury of utilizing by using Qorona:

  • COVID Questionnaire - A questionnaire that makes use of artificial intelligence in order to predict the likelihood of you having the coronavirus, Feel free to try it out here
  • Book vaccinations and have COVID testing kits delivered right to your doorstep. Click here for more info.
  • View a detailed, live map and view the different areas of Qatar and their relative population densities. Make better decisions on where to head to using this map.
  • A clean and modern user interface which makes all of our features available at the tip of your finger.



Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)


How do I protect myself against COVID-19?
  • Always wear a mask (N-95 preferred) if you're outdoors.
  • Keep outdoor gatherings limited to 15 people or less.
  • Wash sanitize your hands frequently.
  • Practice social distancing and try to keep a distance of 6 feet between you and other people.
  • Keep up to date with the official government's rules and regulations regarding COVID-19.

How does the statistics map work?

Whenever someone enters or leaves a major attraction or location in Qatar, they will have to scan their Qorona code on a QR scanner, This scanner updates our servers with a rough estimate of the population at any given place.


What do the different colors of the Qorona code indicate?
  • Green: : Healthy
  • Red: Exposed to COVID-19
  • Grey: Suspected
  • Yellow: Quarantined
