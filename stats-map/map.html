Statistics map

The statistics map is a significant, brand new development which takes the concept of maps and extends their functionality by using artificial intelligence in order to produce a population density map but with a twist - itâ€™s live and it predicts future population densities based on previous data. How? The Qorona project hopes to install small, portable QR code scanners at every major attraction and location in Qatar. Why? On entry and exit, users will be forced to scan their QR code on the scanners, which allows us to track the amount of people at any one place. This provides a major advantage over other competitors (mainly Ehteraz) - people are able to make more informed decisions by checking the map which highlights highly populated areas in a shade of red, and sparsely populated areas in a shade of green.
